Churchogouge (Church-O-Gouge)

a shared house of worship where both Christian and Jewish services are held.

Doose's Market

The main market/grocery store in Stars Hallow runned by local, Taylor Doose.

Miss Patty's

A dance school runned by Stars Hollows, Mrs. Patty, it is also the venue for several fundraisers/events and Town Meetings.

The Dragonfly Inn

Formerly a bed-and-breakfast owned by the Weston's and purchased by Loreli Gilmore (executive manager) and Sookie St. James (head chef). A wonderful place to stay with a fantasic dining services, along with activities for the whole family to enjoy any time of year.

Stars Hollow Museum

This is a community runned museum that displays historical artifact of the towns history.

The Gazebo

The center of Stars Hollow is located right under the town's gazebo. If you are walking around town and you take 3 lefts you will be right back at the center of town.

Edgar Allen Poe Society

This annual event is one of stars hollows biggest litterary tourism events. Men come to stars hollow and dress up as Edgar Allen Poe and recite and renact his poems.

Firelight Festival

Every late winter and early spring,the town comes together to celebrate The Firelight Festival celebrating the founding of our beloved town, Stars Hollow.

Movie in the square

One a month the town hosts a movie in the square which is held in the town square, and the movie is chosen by the citizen of the month.

Revolutionary War Reenactments

Every year the Stars Hollow Historical Society comes together and reenacts the battle of how stars Hollow came to be. this event is fun for families and kids of all ages.

Al's Pancake World

A restaurant in Stars Hollow, that used t be a pancake house, but now serves international cousine.

Luke's Diner Town Favorite

Owned and run by Stars Hollow local, Luke Danes.This diner is open for breakfast, lunch, dinner and is very old fashioned and makes you feel right at home. There is only one rule to follow which is, No phones allowed.

Taylor's Old Fashioned Soda Shoppe

Here you can find all the candy, milkshakes, hot chocolate, and ice cream soda of all flavors you could possibly imagine. This buiseness is owned by Stars Hollows own, Taylor Doose.

Weston's Bakery

This business was orgionally established in 1955 by Fran Weston. This is Stars Hollows local bakery that serves over 32 types of pies, cakes, scones, muffins, coffee, tea, and hot chocolate.
