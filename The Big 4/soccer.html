SOCCER

2019-20 Varsity Team

In the 2019-2020 Soccer season, this is the best season the lady lynx soccer team has ever had. This year the soccer team will be graduating 3 seniors and will continue to get stronger. They fell 6-0 to Battle Ground Academy on their road to state. We are proud of yall!

Also, this season Taylor Rhodes '21, Jordan Timmons '21 and Analise Murphy '22 were all honored and added to the West District Team. Rhodes and Simi Faleye '22 were named to the Regional Tournament Team. With head coach Amy Morrison was awarded Coach Of The Year.

August 20, 2019

Lausanne vs. St. Georges

After a rough preseason, the lady lynx first faced the lady gryphons of St. Georges. The lady lynx put up a rough fight, but fell to the St. Georges with a score of 1-7.

September 5, 2019

Lausanne vs. Nighthawks

Next, the lynx faced the Memphis Nighthawks. In this non-confrence game the lady lynx showed off the hard work they put in during the summer, and won with a score of 7-0.

September 7, 2019

Lausanne at Northpoint

First away game of the season, the lynx took on the trojans of Northpoint. The lady lynx grinded it out and showed, Northpoint had nothing on them. They won with a final score, 9-0.

September 12, 2019

Lausanne vs. Hutchison

Back at the grind house, the ladies next took on the Hutchison sting. The ladies played head to head, but in the end Lausanne continue their winning streak. The final score was 3-2.

September 24, 2019

Lausanne vs. White Station

Now on a 3-game winning streak the lynx will take on the White Station spartans. In this non-conference game the lady lynx fell to the spartans. The final score of the game was 2-5.

September 26, 2019

Lausanne at Harding Academy

The lady lynx will now face the lions at Harding Academy in their second away game. In this district game the lady lynx defeated the lady lions, 5-1. Now the lady lynx are 2-1 in the district.

October 1, 2019

Lausanne vs. Germantown

The lady lynx next faced the Germantown Red Devils. In this non-conference game the lady lynx showed off all the hard wok they have put in, and came out with the dub. The final score of the game was 5-0.

October 3, 2019

Lausanne vs. Munford

Wrapping up the season, the lady lynx on a 3-game winning streak will face Munford. In the non-conference game, the lady lynx grinded and came out with the win, 4-3. Great reguar season ladies!
