BASKETBALL

Varsity Lady Lynx

This year's basketball team is described by head coach Mears as "young, determined, and hungry." The lady Lynx return with only one starter senior Kayla Pointer class of '20. The team prepares to welcome four more members to the Lausanne team and family: Clinne' Caples class of '21, Kai Wince class of '22 Abby Hough class of' 23,and Curea Cunnigham class of '23. The Lady Lynx basketball team is lead by Head Coach Lesa Mears and Assistant Coach Tracy Summers.

Lady Lynx, GEN Z

The lady lynx basketball team has a brand new look to it for the 2019-2020 season. Not only do they have some new fresh faces, they also will be receiving new look and style. Head Coach, Lesa Mears is very excited for this upcoming season, and says "there is so much talent and passion amongst these ladies."

A Look at the Team

This years lady lynx basketball team consists of: three seniors Kayla Pointer '20, Logan Segal '20, and Abby Martin '20, one junior Clinne' Caples '21, three sophomores Kai Wince '22, Simi Faleye '22,and Maya Campbell '22, and three freshman Lucy Fisher '23, Abby Hough '23, and Curea Cunnigham '23.

Saying Goodbye
