TRACK AND FIELD

Track and Field is one of the oldest sports, these contests were often held at religous festivals and also were apart of the Greek Olympic Games. It wasnt until the 1860s that the sport began to truly. In 1866 the first English championships were held by the newly formed Amateur Athletic Club. All races started by the firing of the gun by an official at the starting line.For races up to and including one lap of an outdoor track, runners must stay in their lane. There are usally six to eight lanes and winners are based on whose torso first crosses the finish line.

Track Spikes 

Track Spikes are racing shoes used by athletes when racing on the track. Some spikes are designed for longer term training on tracks, but generally the shoes are used for racing, found at Nike.

Equipment 

This is a website for coaches and athletes, where they can purchase track and field equipment needed. There is  equipment such as hurdles, blocks, and relay batons. Athletes have to get thier own shoes and spikes.
