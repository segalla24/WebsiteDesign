About

Stars Hollow was founded in 1779, and named from a legend involving two-star crossed lovers who were drawn together at the spot where our town currently exists. The town is styled after the Revolutionary War era, and anually celebrate a battle that never acually occured. It is the home to the many friendly, eccentric characters, charming New England scenery, with its ever changing history, you'll find Stars Hollow is the Heart of Conneticut. Being 30 miniutes outside Hartford we are located near many other towns and attractions.

Citizens of the Month: Loreli and Rory Gilmore

Loreli is the current manager of the Dragonfly Inn and her daughter, Rory, is currently incharge of the Stars Hallow Gazete. In their free time, you can always find them drinking a cup of coffee at Luke's, and never miss one of the town meetings. They have been proud residents of Stars Hollow for over 20 years. Thank you for all of your contributions to our small beloved town Loreli and Rory.
